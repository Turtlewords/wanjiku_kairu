ABOUT ME

Height: 5"5 | Eye & Hair Color:

Dark Brown | Vocal Range: C3-A5

Born in New Jersey, I moved to Chicago to pursue an education in improv, musical improv and comedy writing at The Second City. I have since completed comedy programs at Annoyance Theatre, ComedySportz, and Musical Comedy Live. I have performed with improvised musical group Baby Wants Candy, model and act in short films, national commercials, and web-series. I am currently an understudy for Second City Mainstage 112th Revue.
